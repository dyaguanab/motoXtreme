BORN ON THE RACETRACK

Below is a presentation of the various racing cars produced by brands that specialize in manufacturing motorcycles. This overview highlights the unique designs and engineering prowess of these companies, showcasing their dedication to excellence in both automotive and motorcycle industries.

REVOLUTIONARY. PURISTIC. RADICAL.

The production KTM X-BOW is KTM’s interpretation of a super sports car for the 21st century. "We took Colin Chapman's idea of a spartan, lightweight sports car reduced to the bare essentials and transfered it into the new millennium – with as many technological innovations as possible", says KTM CEO Stefan Pierer, of the first four-wheeled model in KTM’s history


INNOVATIVE. RELIABLE. VERSATILE.

The Honda Civic represents our vision of a modern, versatile vehicle designed to meet the needs of the 21st-century driver," says Honda CEO Takahiro Hachigo. This overview highlights the Civic's distinctive design and advanced engineering, showcasing Honda's dedication to excellence in both form and function. By integrating cutting-edge technology and maintaining a focus on sustainability, the Honda Civic continues to set the standard in the automotive industry.
